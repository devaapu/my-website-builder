About Sytaak

The future of AI-powered e-commerce is here. Discover what makes us different.

Our Mission

At Sytaak, we're revolutionizing e-commerce by empowering store owners with cutting-edge AI automation and design tools. Our mission is to make professional online selling accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skills.

We believe AI should handle the heavy lifting - from store setup to product descriptions, marketing automation to customer service - so you can focus on what truly matters: growing your business.

AI-powered automation that saves you hours every day

Our Story

2019 - The Idea

Our founder recognized the growing complexity of e-commerce platforms and saw an opportunity to simplify store creation using emerging AI technologies.

2020 - First Prototype

After a year of research and development, we built our first AI-powered store builder that could create complete Shopify stores in under 10 minutes.

2021 - Beta Launch

We launched our beta to select users who helped us refine the platform, resulting in a 92% satisfaction rate from early adopters.

2022 - Public Release

Sytaak officially launched to the public, offering features that surpassed traditional platforms at a fraction of the cost.

2023 - Today

With thousands of happy customers, we continue to innovate, adding new AI features monthly to keep our users ahead of the competition.
